Operational Process

Green Logistics' Same-Day Delivery service is optimized for Shops requiring speed:

  • 08:00 - 10:30: Shipper picks up goods at your door.
  • 10:30 - 12:00: Goods arrive at central hub, automatic sorting.
  • 13:00 - 14:00: Dispatched for delivery.
  • Before 18:00: Customer receives the package.
